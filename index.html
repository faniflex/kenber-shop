About Kenber

Pioneering smart irrigation solutions for Ethiopian agriculture since 2022.

Kenber Smart Irrigation Systems is an Ethiopian company dedicated to bringing modern, water-efficient irrigation technology to farmers across the country. Our mission is to help Ethiopian farmers increase their yields while conserving precious water resources through smart, automated irrigation solutions.

All our products are designed with Ethiopian farming conditions in mind and are supported by our local team of agricultural technology experts. We understand the unique challenges of farming in Ethiopia and have tailored our solutions accordingly.
